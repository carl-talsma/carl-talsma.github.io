About Me

I’m a Research Scientist and data professional focusing on low-carbon energy R&D and software, including CO2 capture and storage (CCS), methane detection and quantification, hydrology, and applications of machine learning. I’m currently working at Carbon Solutions where we build software to optimize energy infrastructure and plan for the energy transition. Previously, I worked at Los Alamos National Laboratory in the Computational Earth Science group where I studied how hydrology changes under a changing climate. I’m interested in using satellite and climate data to deliver real world impacts.
